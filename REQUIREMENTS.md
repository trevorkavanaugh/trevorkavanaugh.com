# Project Requirements

## Business Requirements

### Core Business Information
- **Company Focus:** Bank Risk Management Consulting
- **Specialization:** Third Party Risk Management
- **Value Proposition:** "Building Risk Management Frameworks That Scale"

### Target Audience
- Financial institutions
- Banks and credit unions
- Regulated financial entities
- Risk management departments
- Compliance officers

### Key Messages
- Expertise in third-party risk management
- Scalable frameworks (not one-size-fits-all)
- Banking industry specialization
- Professional, trustworthy service

## Design Requirements

### Visual Style
- **Overall Aesthetic:** Professional, corporate, authoritative
- **Industry Alignment:** Banking/financial services standards
- **Tone:** Conservative, trustworthy, expert

### Color Palette
- **Primary Colors:** Navy blues (various shades)
- **Secondary Colors:** Greys (light grey to charcoal)
- **Accent Colors:** Tans, warm neutrals, possibly subtle gold
- **Contrast:** High contrast for text readability

### Layout Requirements
- Clean, uncluttered design
- Professional whitespace usage
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Modern but not trendy (timeless design)

## Functional Requirements

### Essential Pages (Priority Order)
1. Homepage - Primary entry point, overview of services
2. Services/Expertise - Detailed service offerings
3. About - Credentials, experience, background
4. Contact - Inquiry form, contact information

### Navigation Requirements
- Clear, intuitive menu structure
- Consistent navigation across all pages
- Mobile-responsive menu
- Easy access to contact/inquiry

### Content Requirements
- Clear service descriptions
- Credibility indicators (experience, credentials)
- Case studies or success metrics (if available)
- Clear call-to-action elements

## Technical Requirements

### Performance
- Fast load times (< 3 seconds)
- Optimized images and assets
- Minimal external dependencies

### Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader compatibility
- Proper semantic HTML

### Responsiveness
- Mobile-first or mobile-friendly design
- Tablet optimization
- Desktop optimization
- Common breakpoints: 320px, 768px, 1024px, 1440px

### Browser Support
- Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Graceful degradation for older browsers

## Constraints and Considerations

### Budget Considerations
- Starting as a solo consultancy
- May need cost-effective hosting solution
- Prefer simple, maintainable solutions

### Timeline
- Iterative development approach
- Phase-by-phase delivery
- Flexibility for refinement

### Maintenance
- Easy to update content
- Minimal technical dependencies
- Clear documentation

## Nice-to-Have Features
- Blog or insights section
- Client testimonials
- Newsletter signup
- Resource downloads (whitepapers, guides)
- LinkedIn/professional network integration

## Out of Scope (Initially)
- E-commerce functionality
- Client portal/login
- Complex interactive tools
- Multi-language support
