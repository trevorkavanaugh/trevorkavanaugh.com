# Design Option 2: Modern Professional

## Overview
Contemporary design with clean lines and strategic color accents. Balances professionalism with approachability and modern aesthetics.

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Navy Blue `#1D3557`
- **Secondary:** Slate Grey `#4C607B`
- **Accent:** Coral Red `#E73A46`
- **Background:** White `#FFFFFF`
- **Light Grey:** `#F8F9FA`
- **Text:** Charcoal `#2D3436` (body), Navy `#1D3557` (headings)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat (modern sans-serif)
  - H1: 56px, Bold, Navy
  - H2: 40px, Semibold, Navy
  - H3: 28px, Semibold, Slate Grey
- **Body:** Inter or System UI (clean sans-serif)
  - Body: 18px, Regular, Charcoal
  - Line height: 1.7

### Visual Style
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Flat design with strategic shadows
- Contemporary photography (dynamic, professional)
- Iconography: Filled or duotone style
- Strategic use of coral accent for CTAs and highlights
- Asymmetric layouts for visual interest

## User Experience

### Navigation Behavior
- Sticky header with subtle shadow on scroll
- Smooth scroll to sections
- Active page: Coral underline
- Mobile: Slide-in menu from right

### Interactive Elements
- **Primary Button:** Coral background, white text, slight shadow
  - Hover: Darker coral, larger shadow
- **Secondary Button:** Navy outline, navy text
  - Hover: Navy background, white text
- **Links:** Navy text, coral underline on hover
- **Cards:** White background, subtle shadow
  - Hover: Coral left border, lifted shadow

### Animations
- Subtle fade-in on scroll for sections
- Button hover transitions
- Smooth page transitions
- Card hover effects

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
- Wide: > 1440px

## Content Tone

### Writing Style
- Professional but conversational
- Clear, direct language
- Client-focused ("you" language)
- Results-oriented
- Transparent about process and pricing

### Key Messages
1. Specialized expertise without enterprise overhead
2. Direct access to senior expertise
3. Flexible, scalable engagements
4. Proven results for similar institutions
5. Independent, objective perspective

## Pros of This Design
- Modern, contemporary feel
- Distinctive and memorable
- Approachable while professional
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Strategic use of accent color draws attention
- Balances corporate and boutique aesthetics

## Cons of This Design
- Coral accent may be too bold for some conservative clients
- More design maintenance required
- Could feel less traditional to very conservative audiences

## Best For
Consultants who want to:
- Stand out from larger, traditional firms
- Appeal to modern banking professionals
- Show expertise with approachability
- Attract mid-size banks and credit unions
- Position as innovative but credible
