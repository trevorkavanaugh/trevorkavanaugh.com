Advisory

Third-party risk advisory through Provenance Risk Advisory, LLC

My advisory practice operates through Provenance Risk Advisory — a governance-first third-party risk advisory firm founded on 14+ years of operational experience managing vendor portfolios in regulated financial environments. I built and ran these programs. I know what holds up under regulatory scrutiny and what falls apart under real-world pressure.

This site is where I publish research, insights, and perspectives on third-party risk management. Provenance is where the advisory work happens — program design, architecture reviews, readiness assessments, and training engagements.
