Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

Overview

This website (trevorkavanaugh.com) is a personal thought leadership site focused on third-party risk management. I respect your privacy and am committed to being transparent about how this site operates.

The short version: This site collects standard web analytics to understand how visitors use the site, offers an optional newsletter subscription, and does not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information Collection

This website collects information in the following ways:

  • Newsletter subscription: If you subscribe to the newsletter, your email address is collected and stored to send you articles and updates. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.
  • Article comments: If you submit a comment, your name and comment content are collected and stored.
  • Analytics data: Described in detail below.

This website does not:

  • Require user accounts or registration to read content
  • Process payments or transactions
  • Sell your data to third parties

Analytics

This site uses analytics to understand how visitors interact with content. This helps improve the site and create more relevant articles. The following data is collected:

  • Device information: Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet), browser, operating system, screen resolution, language preference, and timezone
  • Browsing data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, scroll depth, referring website, and click interactions
  • Location: Approximate geographic location (country, region, city) derived from your IP address using GeoIP lookup. Your IP address is not stored.
  • Session data: A randomly generated visitor ID stored in a cookie to understand return visits and session duration

This site also uses Google Analytics. For information on how Google processes data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: A cookie stores a randomly generated visitor ID to track sessions and return visits. This cookie does not contain personal information.
  • Google Analytics: Google may set cookies to collect analytics data.

Your browser settings allow you to control cookie preferences. Disabling cookies may affect analytics accuracy but will not prevent you from using the site.

Hosting and Technical Data

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit, GitHub may automatically collect standard server logs including IP address, browser type, and pages visited. For more information, see GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 2 years to identify long-term trends. Newsletter subscriber information is retained until you unsubscribe. Comments are retained indefinitely unless you request removal.

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to external websites, including LinkedIn and other resources. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and I am not responsible for their content or practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to request information about any personal data collected about you, request correction or deletion of your data, and opt out of analytics tracking by disabling cookies in your browser. If you have questions about your data or wish to exercise these rights, please contact me via email.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach me at:

trevor@trevorkavanaugh.com
