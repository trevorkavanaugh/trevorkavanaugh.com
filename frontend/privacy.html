Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Overview

This website (trevorkavanaugh.com) is a personal thought leadership site focused on third-party risk management. I respect your privacy and am committed to being transparent about how this site operates.

The short version: This site collects minimal data, doesn't require accounts, doesn't sell anything, and doesn't share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information Collection

This website does not:

  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Collect personal information through forms
  • Process payments or transactions
  • Store personal data in databases

Hosting and Technical Data

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit, GitHub may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

This data is collected by GitHub as part of their standard hosting service. For more information, see GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Cookies

This website itself does not set cookies. However, GitHub Pages may use cookies for operational purposes. Your browser settings allow you to control cookie preferences.

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to external websites, including LinkedIn and other resources. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and I am not responsible for their content or practices.

Analytics

This site does not currently use analytics tracking tools like Google Analytics. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Your Rights

Since this site collects minimal data, there is little personal information to manage. However, if you have questions about your data or wish to contact me regarding privacy matters, please reach out via email.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach me at:

trevor@trevorkavanaugh.com
