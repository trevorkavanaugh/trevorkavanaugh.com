# LinkedIn Post Topics

Track upcoming post ideas, core messaging, and status through the content pipeline.

## How to Use

Add topics as they come to you. When ready to draft, update status to `drafting`. Once published via `linkedin publish`, update status to `published` and fill in the date.

## Column Guide

| Column | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Topic** | Working title or short phrase |
| **Core Message** | The one takeaway the reader should leave with — what's the point? |
| **Category** | `soc-2` · `supply-chain` · `cloud-risk` · `tprm-ops` · `regulatory` · `hot-take` · `lessons-learned` |
| **Hook Angle** | How to open the post — contrarian take, real-world scenario, question, surprising stat, etc. |
| **Source/Inspiration** | What sparked this — a client situation (anonymized), news event, industry report, prior article, GARP panel topic, etc. |
| **Status** | `idea` → `drafting` → `review` → `published` |
| **Published** | Date posted (YYYY-MM-DD) |
